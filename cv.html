About me

I program since '89, I work partial time as developer since '96, and as full-time developer since '98.

In the course of time I’ve noticed that as a project advances the assurance of software quality without automated tests gets harder and harder.

As quality assurance and improvement in software development is accomplished not only by automated tests I strived towards continuously educating myself, reading about Clean Code, Refactoring, Continuous Integration, Scrum and Software Quality Metrics to only name a few.

The introduction of several agile practices initiated by me at a company I worked for lead to stabilization of development process in such a way that after execution of a build a general statement about the condition of the produced software could be made.

To extend the exchange of experience I joined the Java User Group Münster in summer '09. There I regularly attended talks and held a couple of talks myself, i.e. about Caching with Memcached, Swing and Test Driven Development.
